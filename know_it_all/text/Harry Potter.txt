Harry Potter is a wizard.  
He goes to a school called Hogwarts.  
His parents were killed by the evil Lord Voldemort.  Only Harry survived.  At Hogwarts Harry is in the house Griffindor.  
Harry is the seeker on his house Quidditch team.  
He plays Quidditch on a broomstick.  Harry's best friends are Ron and Hermione.  Harry doesn't like Draco Malfoy.
